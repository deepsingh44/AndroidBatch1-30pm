Android Introduction

Android is a mobile operating system. The Android applications can be built using Java, Kotlin and Flutter. The Android framework helps us to develop advanced and user-friendly applications. History of an Android It is an operating system developed by Android Inc. Android Inc. was developed in Palo Alto California, in October 2003 by Andy Rubin In 2005, Google acquired Android Inc, in 2007. The first version was released by Google and the commercial version was released in 2008 known as Android 1.0. Google acquired Android Inc and later the OHA (Open Handset Alliance). The OHA was established on 5 November 2007, led by Google, including mobile handset makers, application developers, some mobile carriers and chip makers. The OHA's main product is the Android platform - the world's most popular smartphone platform.
